Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your account and personal data from Cham App, you can request it at any time.

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Send us an email at chamroeunoudam@gmail.com with the subject line Request for Data Deletion.
  2. Include your Facebook account name or the email address you used to log in.
  3. We will delete your data from our system within 7 business days and confirm once it has been completed.
